Our Services

At R.D. Industry, we specialize in delivering high-quality steel and stainless steel fabrication solutions to meet all your residential, commercial, and industrial needs. With a commitment to craftsmanship and durability, we offer a wide range of services, including the manufacturing of steel windows, gates, roofing, rolling shutters, staircases, and custom stainless steel works. Our expertise extends to all types of fabrication work, ensuring precision, reliability, and aesthetic appeal in every project. Whether you're looking for robust security solutions or custom designs, R.D. Industry is your trusted partner for excellence in fabrication.

About Us

Founded in 2005 by V. Ravi Kumar, R.D. Industry has grown into a trusted name in steel and stainless steel fabrication, with over 30 years of hands-on experience in the field. Since our inception, we have proudly served more than 5,000 satisfied customers, consistently receiving positive feedback for the quality and reliability of our work. Our extensive expertise spans the manufacturing of steel windows, gates, roofing, rolling shutters, staircases, and all types of custom fabrication projects. At R.D. Industry, we are committed to excellence, delivering durable and aesthetically crafted solutions that meet the highest standards. R.D Industries has been serving the industrial market for over 25 years, delivering top-notch products and services.
